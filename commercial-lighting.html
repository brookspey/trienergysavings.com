Commercial Lighting in West Palm Beach, Florida

Tri Energy Savings offers tailored commercial lighting services that enhance both efficiency and aesthetics for businesses in West Palm Beach, Florida and beyond. Our team works with business owners and facility managers to design and implement lighting solutions that reduce energy costs, improve visibility, and create inviting environments.

Parking Lot Lighting

Safety, security, and visibility for your parking areas with energy-efficient LED solutions. Well-lit parking lots deter crime, reduce liability, and create a welcoming first impression for customers and employees. Our LED parking lot lights deliver superior illumination while consuming significantly less energy than traditional HID fixtures.

Showroom Lighting

Bright, attractive lighting that showcases your products and creates an inviting customer experience. The right showroom lighting can dramatically influence purchasing decisions. We design lighting plans that highlight your merchandise, create depth and dimension, and draw customers through your space.

Warehouse Lighting

LED upgrades and intelligent controls for safety, productivity, and efficiency in warehouse environments. Proper warehouse lighting is critical for worker safety and operational efficiency. Our high-bay LED solutions deliver uniform, bright light across large open spaces while reducing energy consumption by up to 70%.

Retail Store Lighting

Bright, welcoming lights and accent lighting to highlight products and drive sales. Retail lighting goes beyond simple illumination — it creates atmosphere, guides customer flow, and spotlights key merchandise. Our retail lighting designs combine ambient, task, and accent lighting for maximum impact.

Art Lighting

Specialized lighting solutions that properly illuminate artwork and displays. Art lighting requires careful consideration of color rendering, beam angles, and light intensity to faithfully represent colors and textures without causing damage to sensitive materials.
