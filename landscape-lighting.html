Landscape Lighting Installation in West Palm Beach & Palm Beach County

Well-designed landscape lighting transforms your outdoor spaces into stunning, functional environments after dark. Tri Energy Savings provides professional landscape lighting design, installation, and maintenance for residential and commercial properties throughout West Palm Beach, Palm Beach Gardens, Jupiter, Boca Raton, and all of Palm Beach County. Whether you want to highlight your tropical landscaping, improve nighttime safety, or create the perfect ambiance for outdoor entertaining, our experienced team delivers energy-efficient LED lighting solutions tailored to your property.

Why Landscape Lighting Matters for Your Florida Property

Landscape lighting is one of the highest-impact improvements you can make to a South Florida home or business. A professionally designed lighting system enhances your property's curb appeal, making architectural features and lush tropical landscaping visible and inviting even after sunset. Studies consistently show that quality outdoor lighting can increase property values by 15 to 20 percent, making it a smart investment for homeowners across Palm Beach County.

Beyond aesthetics, landscape lighting plays a critical role in security. Well-lit exteriors deter trespassing and vandalism, illuminate dark corners and entry points, and help security cameras capture clearer footage at night. For families, pathway and step lighting reduces the risk of trips and falls around pools, patios, and garden walkways.

Florida's year-round warm climate means you spend more time outdoors than residents in most other states. From evening dinners on the patio to weekend pool parties, landscape lighting extends the usability of your outdoor living areas well into the night. With the right lighting design, your backyard becomes a true extension of your home every single evening of the year.

Types of Landscape Lighting We Install

Choosing the right fixtures is essential to achieving the look, functionality, and longevity you want from your landscape lighting system. At Tri Energy Savings, we exclusively install LED landscape lighting because of its superior energy efficiency, long lifespan, and excellent performance in Florida's demanding climate. LED fixtures use up to 75 percent less energy than traditional halogen bulbs and last 50,000 hours or more, dramatically reducing both your electric bill and maintenance costs.

The types of LED landscape lighting fixtures we install include path lights for driveways and walkways, directional spotlights for uplighting trees and architectural features, in-ground well lights for flush-mounted illumination, bollard lights for commercial walkways and garden borders, recessed step lights for stairs and retaining walls, underwater and pool lights rated for submersion, and string lights for patios, pergolas, and outdoor dining areas.

Every fixture we install is selected for durability in South Florida's heat, humidity, and salt air. We work with premium manufacturers to source corrosion-resistant materials including solid brass, copper, and marine-grade stainless steel that will look great and function reliably for years to come.

Our Landscape Lighting Process

We follow a proven five-step process to ensure every landscape lighting project meets our high standards and exceeds your expectations:

  1. Free Consultation and Site Assessment — We visit your property to evaluate the landscape, discuss your goals and preferences, identify key features to highlight, and assess electrical requirements. This consultation is always free with no obligation.
  2. Custom Lighting Design — Based on our assessment, we create a detailed lighting plan that maps out fixture placement, beam angles, color temperatures, and wiring routes. We walk you through the design so you can visualize the finished result before any work begins.
  3. Professional Installation — Our licensed electricians handle the complete installation, including low-voltage wiring, transformer setup, and precise fixture positioning. We take care to minimize disruption to your landscaping and leave your property clean when the work is done.
  4. Testing and Adjustment — After installation, we test every fixture and circuit at night to fine-tune beam angles, brightness levels, and timer settings. We make adjustments on the spot so your lighting looks exactly the way you envisioned.
  5. Ongoing Maintenance — Landscape lighting systems benefit from periodic maintenance. We offer service plans that include fixture cleaning, lamp replacement, timer reprogramming for seasonal changes, and troubleshooting to keep your system running flawlessly.

Landscape Lighting for Florida's Climate

South Florida's climate presents unique challenges for outdoor lighting systems. Constant humidity, salt air near the coast, intense summer heat, and the threat of hurricanes all take a toll on inferior fixtures and installations. At Tri Energy Savings, we design every landscape lighting system with Florida's environment in mind.

We use salt-air resistant fixtures made from solid brass and copper that will not corrode in coastal environments. Our installations are secured and rated to withstand hurricane-force winds, with buried wiring and anchored fixtures that stay in place during severe storms. We select humidity-proof LED drivers and sealed junction boxes that prevent moisture intrusion, and all of our systems use low-voltage wiring for added safety in wet conditions around pools, fountains, and irrigated landscapes.

Frequently Asked Questions About Landscape Lighting

How much does landscape lighting cost in West Palm Beach?

Most residential landscape lighting projects in Palm Beach County range from $2,000 to $8,000 depending on property size, fixture count, and the complexity of the design. Larger estates and commercial properties may require a higher investment. We offer free on-site estimates so you can get an accurate quote for your specific property — call (561) 401-8079 to schedule yours.

Do you use LED landscape lights?

Yes, all of our landscape lighting installations use LED technology exclusively. LEDs last 50,000 hours or more, use 75 percent less energy than halogen alternatives, and perform exceptionally well in Florida's heat and humidity. LED fixtures also produce less heat, which means less stress on your plants and landscaping near the light sources.

How long does landscape lighting installation take?

Most residential landscape lighting installations are completed in one to two days. Larger or more complex commercial projects may take three to five days depending on the scope. We schedule installations at a time that is convenient for you and work efficiently to minimize any disruption to your daily routine.

Do you offer landscape lighting maintenance?

Yes, we provide ongoing maintenance services for landscape lighting systems. Our maintenance plans include bulb and fixture replacement, beam angle and brightness adjustment, timer and photocell programming for seasonal daylight changes, wiring inspection, and full system troubleshooting. Regular maintenance keeps your landscape lighting looking its best and prevents small issues from becoming costly repairs.

Looking to reduce your energy costs further? Ask about our solar panel installation services — power your landscape lighting with clean solar energy. We also offer commercial lighting solutions and LED lighting installation throughout Palm Beach County.
