Professional Landscape Lighting in West Palm Beach

Well-designed landscape lighting enhances the beauty and security of your property while helping you save on energy costs. Tri Energy Savings offers comprehensive landscape lighting services from design and installation to maintenance and repair in West Palm Beach, Florida and the surrounding areas.

We recommend LED lighting for all landscape projects — it's energy-efficient, long-lasting, and low-maintenance. LED landscape lights provide beautiful illumination while using a fraction of the energy of traditional outdoor lighting systems.

Our team creates custom lighting designs for gardens, pathways, outdoor living areas, and architectural features. Every project is tailored to highlight the unique character of your property while maximizing energy efficiency.

Garden Lighting

Showcase your garden's natural beauty after dark with strategically placed uplights, spotlights, and ambient lighting. Highlight trees, shrubs, flower beds, and water features for a stunning nighttime display.

Pathway Lighting

Guide guests safely through your property with elegant pathway lights. From driveways to walkways, we design lighting systems that combine safety with visual appeal for every path on your property.

Architectural Lighting

Highlight the architectural features of your home or building with precision-placed lighting. Emphasize textures, columns, entryways, and unique design elements that define your property's character.

Security Lighting

Deter intruders and increase visibility around your property with motion-activated and strategically placed security lighting. Protect your home and family with a well-lit exterior.

Transform your outdoor spaces with landscape lighting from Tri Energy Savings. Contact us today for a free design consultation and see how professional landscape lighting can elevate your property's curb appeal and security.
