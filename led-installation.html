Professional LED Installation in West Palm Beach, Florida

Tri Energy Savings provides professional LED installation services in West Palm Beach, Florida. Our team handles everything from the initial consultation to the final installation, ensuring a smooth and seamless experience.

We are experts in installing LED lights for both residential and commercial properties. Our turnkey service means you don't have to worry about a thing — we manage the entire process from start to finish.

Residential Installation

Transform your home with expert LED installation. We work with homeowners to design and install energy-efficient lighting solutions that enhance comfort, aesthetics, and savings. From kitchen under-cabinet lighting to whole-home LED upgrades, our team delivers quality results every time.

Commercial Installation

Our commercial LED installation services cover offices, retail spaces, warehouses, parking structures, and more. We understand the unique lighting requirements of commercial environments and deliver solutions that improve visibility, safety, and energy efficiency.

Retrofit Installation

Upgrade your existing lighting infrastructure to LED without the cost and disruption of a complete fixture replacement. Our retrofit installation services are designed to maximize your return on investment while minimizing downtime.

Maintenance

Beyond installation, we offer ongoing maintenance services to ensure your LED lighting systems continue to perform at their best. Our maintenance programs include regular inspections, cleaning, and prompt replacement of any underperforming components.
