Monetizing Outlets Solutions

Tri Energy Savings offers businesses in West Palm Beach, Florida an innovative way to generate revenue through monetizing outlets.

Our monetizing outlets program allows property owners and businesses to turn their electrical infrastructure into a revenue-generating asset.

Whether you manage a commercial property, parking garage, or public facility, we can help you set up and manage monetized electrical access points.

This is an excellent solution for EV charging stations, co-working spaces, and public venues.

How It Works

Our monetizing outlets system transforms standard electrical outlets into pay-per-use access points. Users can access electricity through a simple payment system, either via a mobile app, RFID card, or integrated payment terminal. Each outlet is equipped with smart metering technology that tracks usage in real time, ensuring accurate billing. Property owners receive detailed usage reports and revenue summaries, making it easy to track the return on investment. The system is fully automated, requiring minimal management once installed.

Revenue Potential

The revenue potential from monetizing outlets depends on location, foot traffic, and usage patterns. High-traffic areas such as airports, shopping centers, and parking garages can generate significant passive income. EV charging outlets in particular are in growing demand as electric vehicle adoption increases. Our team provides detailed revenue projections based on your specific property and location. Many property owners find that monetized outlets not only cover the cost of electricity but generate a meaningful profit margin.

Ideal Locations

Monetized outlets work best in locations where people need temporary access to electricity. Parking garages and lots are ideal for EV charging. Co-working spaces benefit from offering pay-per-use power to non-members. Public venues, event spaces, and outdoor markets can provide vendor power access. Apartment complexes can offer common-area charging for residents. Marinas and RV parks are excellent candidates for metered electrical access. We assess your property to identify the highest-value placement opportunities.

Getting Started

Getting started with monetizing outlets is straightforward. Contact our team for a free property assessment, where we evaluate your electrical infrastructure and identify optimal outlet placement. We handle all equipment sourcing, installation, and system configuration. Training is provided for your staff, and ongoing technical support is included. Our team also assists with pricing strategy to maximize revenue while remaining competitive. From initial consultation to generating your first revenue, the process typically takes just a few weeks.
