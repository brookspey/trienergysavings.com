Parking Lot Lighting Solutions in West Palm Beach

Tri Energy Savings provides solar and LED parking lot lighting solutions for safety, security, and visibility in West Palm Beach, Florida.

Well-lit parking areas are essential for customer safety, employee security, and liability protection.

Our solar parking lot lighting solutions reduce energy costs to near zero while providing reliable, bright illumination throughout the night.

We offer both new installations and retrofits for existing parking lot lighting systems.

Solar Parking Lot Lights

Harness the power of the sun with solar-powered parking lot fixtures that operate independently from the grid, eliminating electricity costs and ensuring reliable nighttime illumination.

LED Retrofits

Upgrade your existing parking lot fixtures to energy-efficient LED technology. Our retrofit solutions can reduce energy consumption by up to 75% while improving light quality and coverage.

Smart Controls

Integrate smart lighting controls with motion sensors, timers, and dusk-to-dawn automation to optimize energy usage and ensure lights are on only when needed.

Maintenance Programs

Keep your parking lot lighting in peak condition with our scheduled maintenance programs, including lamp replacement, fixture cleaning, and electrical inspections.

Whether you manage a retail center, office complex, or multi-family property, Tri Energy Savings has the expertise to design and install a parking lot lighting system that meets your safety needs while minimizing energy costs. Contact us today for a free consultation.
