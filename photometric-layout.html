Photometric Layout Services in West Palm Beach

Tri Energy Savings offers advanced photometric layout services in West Palm Beach, Florida, using cutting-edge tools for accurate lighting design.

A photometric layout maps how light will spread across an area, ensuring optimal coverage, minimal dark spots, and compliance with local codes.

Our team uses industry-leading software to create detailed lighting plans before any installation begins.

This service is ideal for commercial properties, parking lots, sports facilities, and any space where precise lighting coverage is critical.

What Is Photometric Analysis

Photometric analysis is the science of measuring light distribution. We calculate foot-candle levels, uniformity ratios, and light-to-dark ratios to ensure every area receives the right amount of illumination.

Our Process

We begin with a site survey, gather measurements and requirements, then use advanced software to model light distribution. You receive a detailed report with fixture placement recommendations before any work begins.

Applications

Our photometric layouts serve parking lots, warehouses, retail spaces, office buildings, sports courts, and outdoor areas. Any space that requires precise, code-compliant lighting benefits from this service.

Benefits

Eliminate guesswork, reduce over-lighting and energy waste, ensure code compliance, and maximize safety. A photometric layout saves money by specifying exactly the right number and type of fixtures needed.

A professional photometric layout ensures your lighting investment delivers maximum value. Contact Tri Energy Savings today for a free consultation and let our team design the perfect lighting plan for your property.
