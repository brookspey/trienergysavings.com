Water Energy Savings Solutions

Tri Energy Savings offers innovative water energy savings solutions in West Palm Beach, Florida, featuring the patented FloSaver device.

The FloSaver Air Reduction & Efficiency Valve can reduce your water bills by up to 20%.

It works by reducing the air volume trapped in water pipes, creating back pressure that compresses gases before passing through the meter chamber.

This improves water meter reading accuracy by measuring only water volume, not air — meaning you only pay for the water you actually use.

How FloSaver Works

The FloSaver device is installed directly on your water line before the meter. It uses a patented air reduction valve that compresses trapped gases in the water supply before they pass through your meter. Standard water meters measure total volume — including air pockets that naturally occur in municipal water systems. By removing this air before metering, the FloSaver ensures you are billed only for the actual water you consume. The installation is non-invasive and does not affect water pressure or flow rate.

Benefits

The FloSaver delivers immediate and measurable savings on your water bills. Most customers see a reduction of 15-20% on their monthly water costs. The device requires no electricity, has no moving parts, and needs virtually zero maintenance once installed. It is environmentally friendly, as reducing metered air volume means less wasted resource billing. The FloSaver pays for itself quickly, often within the first few months of operation, and continues to save money year after year.

Installation Process

Our certified technicians handle the entire installation process. We begin with an assessment of your current water system and metering setup. Installation typically takes less than two hours and does not require shutting off water to the building for an extended period. Once installed, the FloSaver begins working immediately. We provide a full walkthrough of the system and monitor your initial billing cycles to verify the expected savings are being achieved.

Commercial Applications

The FloSaver is particularly effective for commercial properties with high water usage. Hotels, restaurants, apartment complexes, office buildings, and industrial facilities all benefit significantly from reduced water billing. For property managers overseeing multiple units, the cumulative savings can be substantial. We offer volume pricing for multi-property installations and can provide projected savings estimates based on your current water usage data. Contact us for a free commercial water audit.
